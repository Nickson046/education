Why Choose Us

Everything You Need to
Study in Europe

From your first inquiry to your first day on campus, MarinGlobal Scholars walks every step with you.

University Matching

We analyze your academic profile and goals to match you with universities where you'll genuinely thrive — not just where you'll be accepted.

Application Support

Our experts craft compelling applications, review documents, write recommendation requests, and manage deadlines across multiple universities.

Visa Guidance

Navigating EU student visa requirements is complex. We guide you through every document, appointment, and interview preparation step.

Scholarship Access

We connect students with scholarships, grants, and financial aid programs specifically available to African students in Baltic universities.

Pre-Departure Prep

Accommodation guidance, banking setup, health insurance, cultural orientation — we ensure you arrive in Europe confident and prepared.

Ongoing Support

Our relationship doesn't end when you land. We maintain an alumni community and provide continued support throughout your studies.
