Our Mission

Education That Changes Lives

MarinGlobal Scholars was born from a recognition that navigating European university admissions from East Africa is unnecessarily complex. Bureaucratic barriers, language confusion, and lack of guidance prevent many outstanding students from accessing the education they deserve.

We exist to change that — providing expert, end-to-end support that transforms European education from a distant dream into an achievable reality.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted gateway to European higher education.

Our Promise

Honest guidance, genuine care, and measurable results for every student.

Why We Exist

What We're Building

What started as a firsthand frustration — watching talented East African students fail to navigate EU university applications not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked guidance — became the founding idea behind MarinGlobal Scholars.

We are building the consultancy we wish had existed. In 2026, we open our doors with a fully curated Baltic university network, deep on-the-ground knowledge across East Africa, and a commitment to staying with every student from first inquiry to first day of class.

2026

MarinGlobal Scholars Founded

Launched in Riga with a curated network spanning Latvia, Lithuania, and Estonia — and deep roots across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and Ethiopia. Our founding intake is now open.

2026

First Cohort, Full Support

Every student in our founding class receives direct founder-level attention — end-to-end document preparation, visa guidance, and pre-departure support.

2027

Expanding Our Reach

Growing our in-country advisor presence across East Africa — making expert guidance accessible to students in every corner of the region, not just major cities.

2028 & Beyond

The Alumni Effect

Our long-term vision: a self-sustaining network of Baltic-educated East African professionals — mentors, advocates, and living proof of what becomes possible when the right door is opened.
