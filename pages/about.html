Founded with a single conviction: every talented East African student deserves access to world-class European education, regardless of where they start.

Our Mission

Education That Changes Lives

MarinGlobal Scholars was born from a recognition that navigating European university admissions from East Africa is unnecessarily complex. Bureaucratic barriers, language confusion, and lack of guidance prevent many outstanding students from accessing the education they deserve.

We exist to change that — providing expert, end-to-end support that transforms European education from a distant dream into an achievable reality.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted gateway to European higher education.

Our Promise

Honest guidance, genuine care, and measurable results for every student.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

What started as one person's frustration watching talented friends fail to navigate EU university applications has grown into East Africa's leading Baltic study-abroad consultancy.

2018

Founded in Nairobi

MarinGlobal Scholars established with a focus on Latvia, helping the first cohort of 12 students.

2019

Expanded to Lithuania

Added Lithuanian university partnerships, with Vilnius University becoming a flagship partner.

2021

Estonia Partnership

Formalized partnerships with University of Tartu and TalTech, completing the Baltic trio.

2023

100th Student Placed

Celebrated a milestone — 100 students successfully enrolled in European universities.

2025

350+ Students & Growing

Now serving students across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Ethiopia, Ghana, and Nigeria.
