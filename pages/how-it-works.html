Important Note on Visa Requirements

Visa requirements differ slightly between Latvia, Lithuania, and Estonia, and may change over time. Our advisors stay current with the latest consular guidance for all three countries and for students from different African nations. We never give generic advice — everything is tailored to your specific situation.

Entry Requirements

What You Need to Apply

General requirements for most English-taught programs. Specific requirements vary by university and program.

Academic Transcripts

Secondary school and/or undergraduate transcripts. Minimum grade requirements vary (typically B average or equivalent).

English Proficiency

IELTS (6.0–7.0) or TOEFL (80–100) for most programs. Some universities accept other evidence of English fluency.

Work Experience

Required for most Master's programs (1–3 years). Not required for Bachelor's programs in most cases.

Statement of Purpose

A personal essay explaining your motivation, goals, and why you chose this university and program.

Financial Proof

Bank statements or scholarship letters demonstrating ability to cover tuition and living costs (€5,000–€12,000/yr).

References

2–3 academic or professional references. We guide you on who to approach and what to request.
