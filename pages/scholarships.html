Strategy

How to Maximise Your Funding

Our scholarship advisors have helped students secure tens of thousands of euros in combined awards. Here's what works.

01

Apply Early & Apply Wide

Many scholarships operate on a rolling basis. Applying 6–8 months ahead of your intake significantly improves your odds of securing funding.

02

Stack Multiple Awards

Government grants, university merit scholarships, and private bursaries can often be combined. Some of our students cover 80%+ of their costs this way.

03

Craft a Compelling Statement

Scholarship committees want to see your story, your goals, and your community impact. Our team reviews and refines every personal statement.
